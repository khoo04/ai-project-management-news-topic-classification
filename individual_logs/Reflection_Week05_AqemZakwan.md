## 2. How could you reduce costs without affecting quality?

In this digital era, there are a lot of ways to reduce cost:
1. Reuse same existing template or framework.
2. Use free open source software or tools.
3. Plan ahead with the team and proceed the project according to the plan.

## 3. How does resource allocation influence your schedule and overall success?

In AI project management, resource allocation greatly influences the schedule and overall success because the right distribution of time, talent, and tools ensures tasks are completed efficiently.
When skilled team members and adequate computing resources are assigned appropriately, project milestones are achieved on time.
Poor resource allocation, however, can lead to delays, overworked team members, and reduced quality of outcomes.
